✚Step 4: Possible Etiologies

Refine Metabolic Acidosis Etiologies

Calculated serum osmolal gap— mOsm/kg

Serum osmolal gap = measured serum osmolality − (2 × Na + glucose/18 + BUN/2.8).

Calculated urine anion gap (UAG)— mEq/L

UAG = Na⁺ + K⁺ − Cl⁻. Measurement is unreliable with polyuria, urine pH >6.5, and when urinary Na <20 mmol/L.

Refine Metabolic Alkalosis Etiologies

If urine chloride is low (<25 mmol/L), the cause is often saline-responsive. If high (>25 mmol/L), saline-resistant — often due to mineralocorticoid excess.
